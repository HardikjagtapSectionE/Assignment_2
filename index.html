About Us

Welcome to Nirvana Cafe – your digital escape into the world of coffee and code. At Nirvana Cafe, we're more than just a coffee shop. We're a hub of connectivity, creativity, and community nestled in the heart of 2000 Rue Sainte-Catherine. Since 2015, we've been serving up delicious coffee, mouthwatering pastries, and a welcoming atmosphere to locals and visitors alike.

In addition to our delectable coffee and baked goods, we also offer high-speed internet access, making us the perfect destination for digital nomads and tech-savvy individuals. Whether you need to check your email, catch up on work, or simply browse the web, you can count on Nirvana Cafe to keep you connected.

So whether you're dropping by for your morning caffeine fix, settling in for a productive work session, or catching up with friends over a latte, we invite you to make yourself at home at Nirvana Cafe. We can't wait to welcome you!
